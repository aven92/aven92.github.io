# 设置分区为 [GPT](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GUID_Partition_Table)
因为[MBR](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Master_boot_record)限制单盘的最大地址存储空间为2TB

	# parted /dev/sdb
	(parted) mklabel gpt
	(parted) unit s
	(parted) print free
	(parted) mkpart primary 0% 10TB
	(parted) print free
	(parted) quit

# 格式化为ext4

	# mkfs.ext4 /dev/sdb1

# 挂载 
	# mount -t ext4 /dev/sdb1 /mnt
